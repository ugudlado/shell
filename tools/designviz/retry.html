Real-world: AWS SDK uses exponential backoff with jitter when retrying throttled DynamoDB requests. Without backoff, hundreds of clients retrying instantly create a thundering herd that overwhelms the service. Jitter spreads retries over time, preventing synchronized bursts and allowing the service to recover.
